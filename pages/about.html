ABOUT LAST ALBUM

After fascinating the public by displaying various charms through [SQUARE ONE], [SQUARE TWO], and ‘AS IF IT’S YOUR LAST’, YG Entertainment’s representative girl-group BLACKPINK made a fascinating comeback with their first-ever mini album, [SQUARE UP]. BLACKPINK’s new challenges toward musicality and their twofold ‘BLACK’ & ‘PINK’ charms are expected to leave a strong impression on the public.
