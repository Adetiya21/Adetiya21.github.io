JENNIE (제니)

Profile

Fullname:Jennie Kim (제니김)
Position:Main Rapper, Vocal
Birthday:1995, January 16 (Capricorn)
Blood Type:B
Nationality:Korean

Bio

Jennie was introduced as the first member of the upcoming YG girl group “Pink Punk” back in 2012. Between 2012 and 2013, she was heavily featured in various music videos and in features on her senior labelmates’ albums. Jennie lived in New Zealand for five years and is capable of speaking three languages: Korean, Japanese, and English.

ROSÉ (로제)

Profile

Fullname:Roseanne Park/Park Chaeyoung (박채영)
Position:Main Vocal
Birthday:1995, Febuary 11 (Aquarius)
Height / Blood Type:168cm / B
Nationality:Australian

Bio

Rosé was the “new face” who hadn’t yet been officially introduced to the public until the weeks leading up to debut. She was born in New Zealand and when she was eight years old, she and her family moved to Australia. Before joining YG, she attended Canterbury Girls Secondary College and was active in her church’s youth group. After being encouraged by her father, Rosé auditioned for YG in 2012. She beat out 400 people for the audition. She soon then moved to Korea after being chosen to be a trainee under the company. Later on that same year, she was featured on G-Dragon’s album One of A Kind on the track, “Without You.” However, she was never officially credited for her feature until her debut teasers. Rosé can speak English and Korean fluently and she is also skilled at playing the piano and guitar.

17.08.13 - Fantastic Duo 2 (contestant) [EP. 20]

JISOO (지수)

Profile

Fullname:Kim Jisoo (김지수)
Position:Vocal, Visual
Birthday:1995, January 03 (Capricorn)
Height / Blood Type:161cm / A
Nationality:Korean

Bio

Known as YG’s CF bluechip, Jisoo became well-known to the public after being introduced as an actress during her pre-debut period. Jisoo has starred briefly in the KBS drama, The Producers, alongside fellow YG labelmate and former 2NE1 member Sandara Park. She is also known as a Tang Wei look-alike among fans. In addition to being the oldest of the group, she is a well-versed entertainer with skills in dancing, singing, acting. She was also serving as an MC on SBS’s “Inkigayo” alongside GOT7’s Jinyoung and NCT’s Doyoung.

2015 Angel Stone CF
2016 Smart Uniform CF with iKON

LISA (리사)

Profile

Fullname:Lalisa Manoban (ลลิสา มโนบาล)
Birth Name:Pranpriya Manoban (ปราณปริยา มโนบาล)
Position:Main Dancer, Rapper, Maknae
Birthday:1997, March 27 (Aries)
Blood Type:O
Nationality:Thai

Bio

Lisa was the second member introduced to the public. She was the only person accepted to YG during the 2010 YG Audition in Thailand. In 2012, she was introduced through a teaser video called, “Who’s That Girl?” Being the first non-Korean idol in YG, Lisa’s presence has gotten many people curious about her talents. She has been acknowledged for her “elegant body ratio” and her “exotic visual.” Lisa is especially talented in dancing, even YG himself praises her. She knows four languages: Thai, Korean, English, and Japanese. She is also childhood friends with GOT7’s BamBam as they were both part of the dance crew “We Zaa Cool.” In July 2017, she briefly had her own show via OnStyle’s Attraction TV called “LISA TV.”
